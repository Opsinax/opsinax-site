[Customer Intent] Based on Mrs. Highland's requirements for a minimalist kitchen with natural materials and a $45,000 budget, I found these matches:

1. Rivera Design #A287 (94% match on design aesthetic but 134% over current budget)

2. Thompson Renovation #C119 (91% match on design but 102% over current budget)

3. Westlake Project #D045 (87% match on design and within budget)

Let's look at how we could modify the Rivera design to bring it closer to budget while maintaining the aesthetic they want.

[Material Intelligence] The Rivera design uses premium walnut cabinetry ($22,800) and Italian marble countertops ($14,500). Based on the customer's preference for natural materials, you could:

• Substitute white oak cabinetry (savings: $8,400)

• Replace Italian marble with engineered quartz with similar veining (savings: $7,200)

• Keep the minimalist hardware which the customer specified as important

These changes would bring the design within 103% of budget while maintaining a 92% aesthetic match to their stated preferences.
